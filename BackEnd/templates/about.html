What is MedAI!!

MedAI aims to empower users with accessible health information and promote proactive health management.The system utilizes advanced algorithms to process user-input symptoms and outputs critical information, including disease diagnosis, detailed descriptions, precautionary measures, recommended medications, suitable workout routines, and dietary suggestions.
